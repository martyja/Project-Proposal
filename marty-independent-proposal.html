Project: Jenny Marty Portfolio

Overview

I will be creating a personal professional portfolio website on Squarespace. I will be transferring the information on my current portfolio website, jennymartyportfolio.wordpress.com as well as adding information and adjusting the presentation of work. I originally created this website for 307. I have since updated it, but am still not satisfied with the format and design of the website. I specifically want to change how I present my past projects. I want to add more information about each in its own page, which you get to by clicking on a single image on the portfolio page. I’m currently in the process of applying for post-grad jobs, and a professional portfolio website is a must for these applications.

Goals

  • Create a portfolio suitable to show to employers and clients
  • Learn how to use Squarespace
  • Optimize content

Audience

  • Potential employers: HR or recruiters for digital marketing agencies, private businesses, or non-profits looking to hire a designer.
  • Potential clients: Small business owners looking to hire a freelance designer.
